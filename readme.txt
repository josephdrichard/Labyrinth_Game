The objective of this game is to avoid the enemy for as long as possible as your score accumulates.  Try to beat the high scores.

Controls:
	Arrow keys, move;
	Enter, view pathfinding data;
	esc, quit;
	0-9, cast spell; and
	lmb, pause - unpause.
Spells:
	0. slow time;
	1. stun;
	2. new level;
	3. heal;
	4. blink;
	5. invisibility;
	6. blast away walls;
	7. speed;
	8. protect; and
	9. fear.
Enjoy the game.
